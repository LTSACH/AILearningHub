🤖 Zero-Shot Classification with LLMs

Classify without training - just prompt!

💡 What is Zero-Shot Classification?

🎯

No Training Needed

LLMs can classify text without any training examples. Just describe the task in natural language!

⚡

Instant Prototyping

Test ideas immediately. No need to collect labels, train models, or tune hyperparameters.

🔄

Flexible Categories

Change categories anytime by updating the prompt. No retraining required!

📊 When to Use Each Approach?

Scenario Traditional ML BERT Fine-tune LLM Zero-Shot
Have labeled data? ✅ Best choice ✅ Best choice ⚠️ Optional
No labeled data? ❌ Can't use ❌ Can't use ✅ ONLY option
Categories change often? ❌ Retrain needed ❌ Retrain needed ✅ Just edit prompt
Production speed? ✅ <1ms ✅ <1ms ❌ 50-200ms
Cost (1M samples)? ✅ ~$1 ✅ ~$5 ❌ ~$100-500
💡 Key Insight: Zero-shot LLM is perfect for prototyping and when you have no labels. For production with labeled data, fine-tuning (BERT or Traditional ML) is more cost-effective.
