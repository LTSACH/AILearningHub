================================================================================
DATASET INFORMATION
================================================================================

Competition Start Date: September 28, 2012
Dataset Files:
  - train.csv (891 passengers with survival labels)
  - test.csv (418 passengers without survival labels)
  - gender_submission.csv (example submission format)

Historical Context:
The RMS Titanic sank on April 15, 1912 during its maiden voyage.
This dataset contains passenger information used to predict survival.
